How It Works

The DNS integrity checker compares your local DNS results with trusted DNS-over-HTTPS (DoH) servers to detect DNS spoofing, cache poisoning, and man-in-the-middle attacks. If mismatched DNS results are detected, the system triggers the kill switch to protect you.


Checks performed: Queries multiple common domains (google.com, cloudflare.com, etc.) and compares local DNS results with Cloudflare and Google DNS-over-HTTPS servers. Runs every 5 minutes when enabled.
